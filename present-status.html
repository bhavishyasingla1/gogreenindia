The Collapse of Earth's Life-Support Systems

If the Earth were stripped of trees, clean water, energy sources, and arable land, the result would be the rapid and total collapse of the biosphere and human civilization.

Without trees, the atmosphere would lose its primary method of filtering carbon dioxide and replenishing oxygen. This environmental breakdown would be compounded by the lack of clean water, causing widespread fatal dehydration and disease spread.
